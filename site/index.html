01 / DEFAULT

Colored UTF-8

Pipe it, page it, or play ansimation at selectable baud rates. CP437 becomes Unicode with colored ANSI output.

02 / PIXEL PERFECT

Kitty graphics

Preserve bitmap fonts and palettes in Kitty, Ghostty, and iTerm2.

03 / EXPORT

PNG, APNG, and GIF

Turn supported BBS formats into indexed-color PNG files, or preserve ANSI and DDW animation frames in looping APNG or GIF files—without a runtime dependency.

Supported formats .ANS.DIZ.ASC.NFO.MEM.TXT.DDW.ADF.RIP.XB.ZIP
